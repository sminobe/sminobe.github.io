Shoshiro Minobe

Climate variability and ocean–atmosphere interaction

Faculty of Science, Hokkaido University

About

My research focuses on climate variability across a wide range of time scales, with particular emphasis on ocean–atmosphere interaction, extreme events, and decadal variability.

Research

Kuroshio–Oyashio Variability

Investigating variability in the North Pacific and its role in regional climate change.

Extreme Warming (2023–2024)

Understanding mechanisms behind recent unprecedented warming events in the ocean.

Atmospheric Fronts and Midlatitude Dynamics

Analysis of frontal activity and its variability and predictability.

Projects & Activities

External project on climatology and prediction of atmospheric fronts.
I contribute seasonal prediction of frontal frequency.
